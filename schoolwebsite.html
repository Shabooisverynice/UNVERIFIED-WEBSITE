San Remigio National High School

Welcome to San Remigio National High School (SRNHS), where excellence and character building go hand in hand. Our school is located in the heart of the community, offering students a safe and stimulating environment to learn and grow.

At SRNHS, we believe in the holistic development of our students, ensuring they are not only prepared academically but also encouraged to develop socially and emotionally. Our dedicated teachers work tirelessly to create an inclusive and engaging learning environment where every student can succeed.

Our curriculum is designed to cater to diverse student needs, combining traditional academic subjects with vocational and technical training to prepare our students for both higher education and immediate employment. We aim to foster creativity, critical thinking, and leadership in our students to shape them into responsible and innovative citizens.
